Android App

LexiWork

AI-Powered Professional English Learning

Platform Android
Timeline 8 weeks
Team 3 devs, 1 designer

The Challenge

Professionals in Law, Medicine, Business, and Engineering across India needed specialized English training that generic language apps couldn't provide. Our client required a solution addressing field-specific vocabulary, pronunciation challenges influenced by regional languages, and low engagement with traditional learning methods.

The Solution

Bazaz Lab developed LexiWork, a comprehensive Flutter application combining AI personalization with scientifically-backed pronunciation training. We integrated Groq AI to generate field-specific content dynamically while ensuring the platform remained engaging through strategic gamification.

Key Features Delivered

AI-Powered Personalization

Groq API integration delivers vocabulary, scenarios, and recommendations tailored to each user's profession and Mother Tongue Influence patterns.

Complete Pronunciation Lab

42 sounds (13 vowels, 24 consonants, 5 diphthongs) with integrated YouTube demonstrations showing precise tongue/lip movements.

Gamification Engine

Daily login streaks, 15 profession-specific achievement badges, and AI-generated daily challenges drive consistent engagement.

Context-Driven Learning

Realistic workplace scenarios—courtroom dialogues for lawyers, patient consultations for doctors—ensure practical application.

Technical Execution

Built with Flutter for cross-platform deployment, LexiWork leverages Firebase for authentication and real-time data sync, integrates YouTube Player API for video demonstrations, and utilizes Groq AI for dynamic content generation. The clean, modern UI uses the Iconsax icon library for aesthetic consistency.

Results & Impact

LexiWork successfully bridges the critical gap in professional English education for Indian learners. The platform's combination of AI-driven personalization, comprehensive pronunciation training, and engaging gamification provides professionals with practical tools for workplace communication confidence.
